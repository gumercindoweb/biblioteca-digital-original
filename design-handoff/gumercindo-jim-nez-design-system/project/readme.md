# Gumercindo Jiménez — Design System

> El caos, convertido en método. Y el método, en dinero.

A luxury-editorial brand system for **Gumercindo Jiménez**, consultant in *Marketing, Ventas y Negocio*. The system targets a high-paying audience that values authority and restraint — built to feel like *Forbes meets nature*: deep intense green, warm earth neutrals, brass-gold detail, and a serif-forward editorial voice.

---

## 1 · Who this is for

Gumercindo takes businesses that already sell but run on chaos, and turns them into profitable, self-sustaining systems. The positioning, in his words: *"Al caos lo busco, y lo convierto en dinero."* He resolves in practice, not from a textbook, and brings **more humanity to marketing** — fewer formulas, more people.

- **Stage:** Growth — already billing recurring fees, wants to scale past a clear ceiling.
- **Ideal client ("Flavio", 50):** invests heavily, works 24/7, exhausted by his own chaos. Wants order, results, and someone he can *delegate to with confidence*. Charismatic, not technical — responds to presence.
- **Emotion to trigger:** *"Alivio con autoridad"* — relief with authority. Certainty, not curiosity. "This is the person I was looking for."
- **Archetype:** Dominant **El Forajido** (Outlaw) + secondary **El Mago** (Magician).

## 3 · Content Fundamentals (voice & copy)

> **Two audiences, two voices.** The brand serves two very different people. The **consulting** voice (below) is the default. The **books & diarios** line speaks to a B2C personal-growth reader — see §3b. Both are Argentine voseo and share the type/gold structure; the register differs.

The voice is **cercana, divertida, disruptiva y técnica** — close, with edge, precise. It is Argentine Spanish and uses **voseo** ("pedí", "vos", "tenés", "contame"). It speaks **"yo" → "vos"**: first person, direct address. Confident and a little provocative, never corporate.

**Tone in practice**
- **Direct, second-person, imperative.** *"Si tu negocio funciona bajo urgencias, no de estructura… ahí entro yo."* / *"Pedí una auditoría que te dará claridad."*
- **Names the chaos, then the method.** Lead with the client's pain ("urgencias", "caos"), resolve with order, system, claridad.
- **Short, declarative, with rhythm.** Editorial cadence — fragments and em-dashes are welcome. *"Ejecuto, documento, automatizo y escalo."*
- **Value before price.** Always frame the number against the value ("Valorada en $250 · Hoy gratis"). The ideal client haggles by reflex; lead with worth.
- **Authority without arrogance.** Knowing, calm, a touch of edge (El Forajido). Never hype-y, never "gurú".

**Casing & punctuation**
- Headlines: sentence case in serif (never ALL CAPS for headlines — that's the old template).
- Labels / eyebrows: UPPERCASE, tracked (Hanken Grotesk). e.g. `EL CAOS, EN MÉTODO`.
- Em-dashes (—) and italics for emphasis; **bold** sparingly to mark the key phrase in a paragraph.

**Emoji:** ❌ none. The original sites used 🧯✅ — drop them. Authority doesn't use emoji ("una marca de autoridad no puede verse como Duolingo"). Use a hairline rule, a gold tick, or tracked caps instead.

**Do / Don't**
- ✅ *"Convierto el caos en un sistema que funciona con o sin tu presencia."*
- ❌ *"¡🚀 Potencia tu negocio con nuestras soluciones 360°!"* — loud, generic, soulless.

## 3b · Books & Diarios voice (the other audience)

The personal-growth line (**Hay que salir del bar**, **Mata al quejica que llevas dentro**) targets a reader who feels **stuck** — trapped by routine, complaint, comfort. The register shifts from *authority* to **warm, honest accompaniment** (the **Mago** archetype rather than the **Forajido**).

- **Asks, doesn't order.** The unit of copy is a *question*, not a command. *"¿Qué te están diciendo tus quejas?"* / *"¿Qué 'bar' de lo conocido te está costando más caro hoy?"*
- **Validates before it challenges.** *"Este diario no es para que dejes de quejarte. Es para que empieces a escucharte."* Name the feeling as legitimate, then reframe it.
- **Reframe structure.** The recurring move is **Queja → Reencuadre → Paso concreto** (complaint → reframe → small action). Steps are deliberately tiny ("10 minutos, no negociable") to kill the excuse.
- **Intimate, hopeful, permission-giving.** Emotion to trigger: *"Puedo empezar."* Honesty over hype; first-person confession is welcome (*"Yo también me sentí estancado…"*).
- **Emoji:** still avoided inside the books/worksheets themselves (the workbook uses typographic block glyphs ✦ ◎ ◈ ✍ ▣, not emoji). Social captions for the books may use the occasional emoji, like the consulting captions.

Block system used across the workbook (reuse the glyphs + labels): **✦ La esencia · ◎ El espejo · ◈ El ejercicio · ✍ Mi compromiso · ▣ Mi termómetro.**

## 4 · Visual Foundations

**The idea:** sobriety with authority. Minimalism that needs no explanation (Apple, Vogue, Zara, Forbes, Harvard). Break the category code — **intense green where everyone else uses corporate blue.**

### Color
- **Primary — Deep Green** (`--green-700 #11503D`, anchored by a near-black `--green-900/950` for dark sections). Authority + nature. *Verde intenso, nunca verde claro* (light green reads cold/funereal — banned).
- **Metal — Brass/Gold** (`--gold-500 #B89455`). Hairlines, rules, the guarantee seal, fine ornament. Luxury without shouting.
- **Accent — Clay/Rose** (`--clay-500 #BE5A53`). The single warm pop, used sparingly — a nod to the brand's pink/red personality energy. Reserve for the hottest single CTA or a highlight.
- **Neutrals — Warm Earth** (`--bone-50 #F8F5EE` paper → `--ink-900 #15130D`). Always warm; **never cold grey.**
- **Nothing loud.** No neon, no primary-bright, no bluish-purple gradients.

### Type
Serif-forward and editorial. **Cormorant Garamond** (display, high contrast — headlines/statements/numbers), **Spectral** (body, screen-optimized serif), **Hanken Grotesk** (tracked uppercase labels, eyebrows, UI). Headlines sentence-case; emphasis via *italics* and color. See `tokens/typography.css`.

### Space, shape, depth
- **Generous whitespace** is the luxury — sections breathe (64–96px vertical rhythm).
- **Restrained radii**: cards `8px`, controls `4px`. Editorial, not bubbly. No big pill cards.
- **Hairline borders** (`--border-hair`, `--border-gold`) over heavy outlines. A 2px gold **rule** is the signature accent (eyebrows, card tops, dividers).
- **Shadows**: soft, warm, low — elevation whispers (`--shadow-sm`→`xl`, warm-tinted). No hard or colored drop-shadows.
- **Cards**: paper surface, 1px soft border, soft shadow, 8px radius, optional gold top-rule (`accentRule`). Dark variant uses deep green with a faint light border.

### Backgrounds
Warm ivory paper as the default canvas; **deep-green / near-black bands** for authority moments (Sobre mí, CTA, footer); a faint **green-tint** band for social proof. No busy patterns or textures. Imagery (when present) is **warm, editorial, natural** — mountains, earth, presence — mirroring the Instagram feed. Photos sit in restrained-radius frames; deep-green duotone placeholders stand in until real portraits land.

### Motion & states
- **Motion**: calm and confident. `--ease-out` (cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1)), 130–420ms. Fades and small lifts — no bounces, no infinite loops.
- **Hover**: buttons darken to the `-600` tint and gain a soft shadow; cards lift 3px with a larger shadow; links shift to brand green / underline in gold.
- **Press**: subtle scale-down (~0.985) + 0.5px nudge. Never a color flash.
- **Focus**: 3px green focus ring (`--shadow-focus`).
- **Transparency/blur**: only on the sticky nav (88% paper + 10px blur) and the modal scrim (62% ink + 4px blur). Used for hierarchy, never decoration.

## 5 · Iconography

The brand is **type-led, not icon-led** — authority comes from words and space, not a busy icon set. There is **no shipped icon font** and **no emoji**.

- **Primary "icons" are typographic:** a 2px gold rule, a gold tick `✓`, the `+` disclosure in the accordion, restrained arrows `→`, and tracked-caps numerals (`01 / 02 / 03`) for sequence.
- **The GJ symbol** (`assets/logo/gj-symbol-*.png`, `gj-badge-*.png`) doubles as the brand glyph/watermark and favicon.
- **If a line-icon set is needed** (dashboards, feature lists): use a thin, single-weight set — **Lucide** or **Phosphor (Light/Regular)** from CDN, stroked in `--text-muted` or `--gold-600`, ~1.5px. Match the hairline aesthetic; never filled, never multi-color. Flag any addition so it stays consistent.
- **Avoid:** emoji, glossy/3D icons, duotone gradient icons, colored-left-border "feature" chips.
