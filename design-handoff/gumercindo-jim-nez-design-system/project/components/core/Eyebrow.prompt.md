The small tracked-caps line that introduces a section, e.g. "EL CAOS, EN MÉTODO" above a serif headline.

```jsx
<Eyebrow tone="accent">Consultoría de marca</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="onDark" rule={false}>Caso 01</Eyebrow>
```

Tones: `accent` (clay, default), `brand`, `metal` (gold), `muted`, `onDark`. Toggle the leading `rule`.
